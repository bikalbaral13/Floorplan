What it does each step
On every portal, the algorithm asks: does the new left or right vertex tighten the funnel, lie outside on its own side (ignore), or cross the opposite feeler (bend!)? Bending: the opposite feeler's endpoint becomes the new apex; restart the funnel from the portal that produced it.
